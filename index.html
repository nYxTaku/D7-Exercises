Website 1

Home Page

The page is designed in a very minimalist way with no navbar at the top.
It has an autoscrolling feature.
The contents keep scrolling down at a fixed speed
Instead of a navbar at the top, it has one in the center.

Hover Effects

Hovering over one of the options highlights that option,
while the other options remain gray.
No other hover effects/animations were seen.

Expanding content

The option, when clicked,
expands to show related images
according to the title and content

Info Carousel

Upon opening an option from the scrolling content,
user is presented with an image and options to sift through to the next or previous image
using the arrow keys or the buttons at the top.

Website 2

Home Page

Home Page has a small navbar at the top
with navigation options on the left and login and cart options on the right.
No content except for the title in stylised font.
Has an animation when hovered on the small tags

Content

Contents placed one after the other in blocks.
Upon hover it fades in,
and upon click loads a new page with description and images

Navbar Options

Navbar is another way to access the contents provided on the content page.
All the content elements are listed in order.
Has a hover effect where target element's color changes.

Login/Register

On clicking the login button, it loads a new page.
With the login/signup panel on the right side of the page.
Nothing particularly stands out and is a pretty normal setup for a login page.
